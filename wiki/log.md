## [2026-05-28] decision | ADR-003: No-Daemon Rule (Configuration Layer)

Third ADR filed. Document: `wiki/decisions/003-no-daemon-rule.md`. Precise scope: the no-daemon rule applies at OUR configuration layer only. The configuration consists entirely of files + Claude Code configurations; plugins we recommend may run their own daemons (claude-mem's port 37777 worker, etc.) — that's their concern, not ours.
